What is Helium ?

Helium is a chemical element with the symbol He and atomic number 2.It is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic, inert, monatomic gas and the first in the noble gas group in the periodic table. Its boiling and melting point are the lowest among all the elements.

What is Parallax effect ?

The effect whereby the position or direction of an object appears to differ when viewed from different positions, e.g. through the viewfinder and the lens of a camera. "what you see in the viewfinder won't be quite what you get in the photograph because of parallax error"
